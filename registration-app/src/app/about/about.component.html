About This Web App

A simple and efficient MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express, Angular, Node.js) web application
built to request information and manage registrations for Towson University workshops and programs.

Course Context / Background: This app makes it easy for anyone interested in Towson University to request more information about our campuses, workshops, or programs. Whether you're a prospective student or just curious about what we offer, you can quickly fill out a simple form to get the details you need. The form is mobile-friendly, fast to complete, and designed to help you stay connected without the hassle.

On the admin side, the app helps staff keep things organized. They can view submissions, mark requests as complete, update info, or remove entries with just a few clicks.
